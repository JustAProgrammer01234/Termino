1. `$set-join-role <role>` - Tells the bot to assign role to members who join the guild.

2. `$set-join-channel <channel>` - Tells the bot to send welcome messages to <channel>.

3. `$welcome-dm-message <message>` - Tells the bot to dm <message> to members who join the guild.

4. `$remove-join-role` - Tells the bot to stop assigning role to users who join the guild.

5. `$remove-join-channel` - Tells the bot to stop sending welcome messages.

6. `$remove-dm-message` -  Tells the bot to stop dming a user who joins the guild.
